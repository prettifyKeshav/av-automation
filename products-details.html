Applications

  • Material Movement: Used for transporting bulk materials across intralogistics operations.
  • Package & Parcel Transport: Ideal for e-commerce, CEP, logistics and 3PL companies to move parcels, totes and boxes.
  • Food Processing: Used in food-grade applications for the transport of packaged goods, bakery items or raw ingredients.
  • Assembly Lines: Integrated into manufacturing plants to transport various stages of assembly or finished products across different stages.
  • Warehouse Automation: Helps in order picking, sorting, and storage systems — efficiently moving goods to packing and dispatch areas.

Industries

  • E-commerce & Retail: Facilitates smooth movement of packages and parcels through distribution/fulfillment centers.
  • Food & Beverage: Ensures hygienic and efficient transport of food products through processing and packaging stages.
  • Apparel & Fashion: Enables fast and accurate movement of packaged garments, accessories and returns through fulfillment centers.
  • Automotive Manufacturing: Supports the transport of components or sub-assemblies on production lines.
  • Pharmaceuticals: Moves packaged products or materials in controlled and clean environments for manufacturing or distribution.
